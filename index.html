Artist Bio

Andrømeda is the creative moniker of Texas artist Harlan Lawson — a rapper, singer, and producer crafting a hybrid sound rooted in atmospheric hip-hop, R&B textures, and pop-driven melodic hooks. His music blends conceptual narratives with autobiographical metaphors, exploring themes of identity, reinvention, and inner transformation.

With a background in both self-production and live performance, Andrømeda builds an immersive sonic universe that feels both intimate and otherworldly. His sets balance high-energy rap moments with emotionally charged melodic peaks, keeping crowds engaged from start to finish while bringing a distinct visual and emotional presence to every stage.
